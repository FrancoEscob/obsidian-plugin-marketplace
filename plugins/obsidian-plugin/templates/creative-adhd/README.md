# Creative ADHD Templates

Templates optimized for creative, non-technical users with ADHD.

## Design Philosophy

These templates are designed around ADHD-specific challenges:

### 1. **Context Blindness**
**Problem:** Forgetting what you were doing when you return to a project.
**Solution:** Every template has a "Next Step" section that's always visible.

### 2. **Overwhelm**
**Problem:** Too many fields or options cause decision paralysis.
**Solution:** Minimal required fields, simple structure, visual clarity.

### 3. **Racing Thoughts**
**Problem:** Ideas come fast and need quick capture before they're lost.
**Solution:** `quick-capture.md` - dump thoughts in seconds.

### 4. **Energy Fluctuation**
**Problem:** Energy levels vary throughout the day.
**Solution:** Every template tracks energy (ğŸ”‹ high, âš¡ medium, ğŸª« low) so you can match tasks to your state.

### 5. **Loss of Progress Awareness**
**Problem:** Hard to see how much progress you've made.
**Solution:** Session summaries auto-track what you accomplished.

## Template Descriptions

### `daily-note.md`
Daily note with:
- Energy check-in (match tasks to energy)
- One main focus (not a huge list)
- Quick wins (small, dopamine-friendly tasks)
- Tomorrow's next step (never start with blank slate)

### `quick-capture.md`
Ultra-fast thought capture:
- No required fields (speed over perfection)
- Just: what's the thought?
- Optional: why does it matter?
- Organize later with `/process-inbox`

### `session.md`
Auto-generated by `session-end` hook:
- What got done
- What's pending
- **EXACT next step** (where to start, what file, what to do)
- Energy needed for next step
- Blockers/questions

This is the KEY file for ADHD users - solves context blindness completely.

### `project.md`
Simple project tracking:
- What/why in plain language
- Main goals (not overwhelming sub-tasks)
- Next tiny step (always visible at top)
- Auto-links to session files
- Blockers section (capture friction immediately)

### `idea.md`
Idea capture and tracking:
- One sentence summary (force clarity)
- Why it's cool (maintain excitement)
- Status checklist (from ğŸŒ± idea â†’ ğŸ“¦ project)
- Easy to link to related ideas
